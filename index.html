About Us

Occupational Therapy

Idam's occupational therapy sessions are geared towards developing essential life skills and promoting independence in daily activities. Our therapists work closely with individuals to address challenges related to self-care, sensory processing, fine motor skills, and social participation.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy program is designed to improve mobility, strength, and coordination for individuals with physical disabilities. Through personalized exercises and interventions, we aim to enhance functional abilities and optimize overall physical well-being.

Speech therapy

Communication is fundamental to social interaction and personal development. Our speech therapy services focus on improving communication skills, language development, articulation, and pragmatic abilities. Through targeted interventions, we help individuals express themselves effectively and engage meaningfully with others.



Special Education

Education is a cornerstone of empowerment. Our special education programs are designed to accommodate diverse learning needs and provide a supportive environment for academic growth. Through differentiated instruction, individualized learning plans, and specialized teaching techniques, we strive to unlock each student's potential and foster a love for learning.

Applied Behavioral Analysis

ABA is a scientifically validated approach to understanding and modifying behavior. At Idam, our ABA programs are tailored to the unique needs of each individual, targeting skill acquisition, behavior management, and social development. We utilize evidence-based strategies to promote positive behavior change and enhance overall functioning.

Mission

Our mission at Idam is to promote inclusivity, independence, and dignity for individuals with autism and disabilities. Through innovative therapies, specialized education, and unwavering dedication, we strive to create a world where every individual is valued, respected, and empowered to thrive.

Vision

Our vision is a society where individuals with autism and disabilities are fully integrated, embraced, and empowered to thrive. We strive for universal inclusivity, upholding everyone's independence and dignity, offering innovative therapies and specialized education for a fulfilling life.
